IAB Thank U

We strongly believe that everyone deserves a future, regardless of their physical abilities. This is why we at IAB strive to create sustainable livelihoods for the visually impaired, to ensure that these youth have a bright life ahead. With this goal in mind, we have undertaken an initiative called Thank U, which helps turn these individuals into entrepreneurs. These aspiring business owners will be in charge of selling healthy yet delicious snacks like cakes, cookies, and savoury items directly to the customer.
